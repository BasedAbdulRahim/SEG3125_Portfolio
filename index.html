About Me

Hi! I'm Based Abdul Rahim, a Software Engineering student at the University of Ottawa (2023–2027), passionate about building technology that’s practical, reliable, and user-friendly. I’m deeply interested in software systems, interface design, and the real-world impact of well-crafted code.

I’ve completed coursework in digital systems, software architecture, UI development, operating systems, and human-centered computing. I’ve also developed strong programming skills in Java, Python, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. My toolbelt includes Git, VSCode, Eclipse, Blender, AutoCAD, and more.

My academic work includes a Java-based AI pathfinding project, a web-based nutrition platform built with modern frontend tools, and contributions to various user interface prototypes. These experiences taught me the value of user-centered thinking, design iteration, and debugging discipline.

Outside of academics, I enjoy gaming, retro visual design, and collaborating with others on creative digital projects. I speak English, French, and Farsi fluently, and bring that same adaptability into every challenge I take on.
